The core Geopogo product lets architects generate massing and layout options
from constraints (site boundary, setbacks, program requirements, target GFA).
It produces multiple candidate designs the architect can compare and refine,
rather than a single answer. The ADA compliance checker is a separate
analysis pass that runs over drawings; the generative engine is about
producing options early in the design process.
