Best results pressing extra-firm tofu 30 min, then marinating in soy, rice
vinegar, garlic, and a little cornstarch before air-frying at 400F for 20 min.
One block (~340g) is about 70g protein split across servings. Pairs well with
edamame to push the protein per meal higher. Tracking this as a staple for
hitting daily macro targets on plant-based days.
