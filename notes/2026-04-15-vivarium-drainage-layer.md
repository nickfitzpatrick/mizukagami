False-bottom approach for the new build: layer of LECA (hydroballs) at the
bottom for water collection, then a mesh screen to keep substrate out of the
reservoir, then the bioactive substrate on top. This prevents the soil from
getting waterlogged and lets excess water pool below the root zone. Springtails
and isopods go in the substrate as the cleanup crew.
